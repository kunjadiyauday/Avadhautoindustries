About Avadh Auto Industries

Based in Gondal, Gujarat, we specialize in radiator coolants and antifreeze blends for passenger cars, commercial fleets, and off-highway equipment. Our formulations balance thermal efficiency with robust corrosion inhibition for multi-metal systems.
